# EgoArena 🎭

**"Your personality is a character. Your character has a record. And the internet decides if you'd survive."**

EgoArena is not just a game; it is a digital mirror. It is a psychological stress-test designed to strip away your curated online persona and expose the raw data of who you actually are.

---

## 👁️ The Vision

In a world of filtered lives and artificial perfection, EgoArena seeks the truth. By forcing you to answer a gauntlet of 10 brutally honest questions, we generate an immutable **Character Card**—a digital footprint of your psychological architecture.

### The Forge
Every response you give is analyzed not just for content, but for intent. The Forge calculates your standing across the five fundamental pillars of the human ego:
- **Discipline**: Your ability to resist the entropy of your own desires.
- **Chaos**: The creative (or destructive) spark of unpredictability you carry.
- **Empathy**: Your capacity to bridge the gap between your self and the "other."
- **Intellect**: The raw processing power of your logical and intuitive mind.
- **Resilience**: Your structural integrity when the world attempts to break you.

### The Fatal Flaw
We don't just highlight your strengths. Every card is etched with a **Fatal Flaw**—the specific behavioral vulnerability that will most likely lead to your eventual undoing. It is sharp, specific, and unsettlingly accurate.

---

## 🔥 The Arena

Once your card is forged, it no longer belongs to you. It belongs to the Arena.

### Psychological Combat
Your identity is pitted against others in real-world scenarios, moral dilemmas, and social stress tests. The world watches. The world judges. 
- Will they trust a **Chaotic Intellectual** to lead them through a crisis? 
- Will they prefer a **Disciplined Empathetic** to rebuild civilization?
- Will your **Fatal Flaw** be your downfall in a 1v1 simulation?

### The Record
Your wins and losses are tracked on the Global Leaderboard. In the EgoArena, your value is determined by the collective consensus of the internet. There are no participation trophies—only the Elo of your ego.
