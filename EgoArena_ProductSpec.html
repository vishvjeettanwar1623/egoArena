01 — Concept

What is EgoArena?

EgoArena turns self-reflection into a competitive game. Users answer 10 brutally honest questions about themselves — their values, habits, flaws, and instincts. An AI processes those answers and generates a Character Card: a styled identity profile with a class, stat bars (discipline, chaos, empathy, cunning, resilience), and a named fatal flaw.

Once your card exists, it enters the arena. Other users vote on hypothetical scenarios — "these two people are stranded together, who leads the group?" — and your card accumulates wins, losses, and a streak. The best cards get showcased. The worst get roasted.

The core loop: answer honestly → get a card that feels unsettlingly accurate → watch strangers judge you → get addicted to your record.

03 — The 10 Questions

The intake form

Questions are designed to be uncomfortable enough to be revealing, but not so heavy that people bounce. They must feel like a game, not a therapy intake form.

1. Your friend is crying. Your first instinct is:

A) Fix the problem / B) Listen / C) Awkwardly offer snacks / D) Fix the problem while they're still talking

2. You are handed €10,000 and 24 hours. What happens?

Free text. AI extracts values (security vs experience vs status vs generosity).

3. What's the lie you tell yourself most often?

Free text. Core input for Fatal Flaw generation.

4. Your group is lost. You:

A) Take charge / B) Follow confidently / C) Follow skeptically / D) Quietly find the exit alone

5. Pick the sentence that describes you:

"I have too many ideas" / "I finish what I start" / "I adapt to whoever I'm with" / "I don't know yet"

6. What do people misunderstand about you?

Free text. Core input for class assignment.

7. Something is unfair. You:

A) Fight it loudly / B) Work around it quietly / C) Accept it / D) Make it work for you

8. At your worst, you are:

A) Controlling / B) Avoidant / C) Chaotic / D) Cold

9. Someone insults you publicly. Your honest reaction:

Free text. Reveals ego, resilience, and cunning score.

10. What would the 10-years-older you be embarrassed you did?

Free text. Final input — used to enrich the Fatal Flaw line.

04 — The Arena

How battles work

Every card can be matched against another in a scenario battle. Scenarios are short hypotheticals. Voters see both cards side-by-side and pick who they think wins. No usernames shown during voting — just the cards.

⚔

Matchmaking

Cards matched by ELO proximity. New cards get 5 placement battles first. Users can also challenge specific cards manually.

🎭

Scenario pool

50+ rotating scenarios across categories: survival, social, moral dilemma, creative, leadership. AI generates new ones weekly.

📊

Voting window

Each battle collects votes for 24 hours. Minimum 10 votes to count. Result shown with breakdown: % voted for each card.

🔥

Streak system

Win 3 in a row → "On Fire" badge. Lose 3 in a row → "Humbled" badge. These reset and create re-engagement loops.

Example scenario prompts shown to voters
"These two are the last people on Earth. Who rebuilds civilization?"

Favors high Discipline + Resilience. Chaos stat is a wildcard.

"A startup of 4 people — who do you want as your co-founder?"

Voters see both cards, pick one, optionally leave a 1-line roast.

"You're stuck in an airport for 12 hours with one of them. Who?"

Light social scenario. Empathy + Chaos are the deciding stats here.

05 — User Flow

End-to-end experience

01

Land on homepage

Hero shows live battle happening in real time. Counter of total cards created. CTA: "Create your card."

02

Answer 10 questions

Mix of MCQ and short free text. Estimated 4–6 minutes. No account required to start — email gate only at the end before card is saved.

03

AI generates your card

Loading screen with "Analysing your psyche..." vibes. Card revealed with animation. User can re-roll one time (triggers re-generation).

04

Share or Enter arena

Card is shareable as an image (OG card). Option to keep card private (visible in battles but no public profile link) or go public.

05

Vote on others

Users can vote without having a card. This drives traffic and brings in non-participants who eventually create their own card.

06

Check your record

Dashboard shows win/loss/streak, recent battle results, how voters described you in roast comments, and your ELO trend over time.

06 — AI Design

Claude API usage

Two distinct AI calls. Both use Claude Sonnet.

Call 1 — Card Generation

Input: all 10 answers. Output: strict JSON with class, alignment, 5 stat scores (0–100), and fatal_flaw string. System prompt enforces tone — sharp, specific, slightly uncomfortable, never generic.

JSON output ~800 token output One-time per user

Call 2 — Scenario Generation

Weekly batch job. Claude generates 20 new scenario prompts with metadata (which stats they favor). Stored in DB. Not a live call — keeps costs near zero at scale.

Batch job Cron weekly Stored in DB

Fatal flaw is the magic. Prompt instructs Claude to extract a specific, embarrassing, but non-judgmental behavioral pattern from Q3 and Q10. Not "you're selfish" — more like "you will apologize sincerely and then do the same thing again within 48 hours."

10 — Monetisation

How to make money

Free tier

1 card, participate in battles, vote unlimited. Sufficient to get hooked.

Pro — $4/mo

3 alternate cards (different moods), detailed battle analytics, custom card theme, remove watermark on share image.

Card frames

One-time cosmetic unlocks. Rare, Legendary, Cursed frame styles. $1–3 each. No gameplay advantage.

Group battles

"My team vs your team" — offices, friend groups, Discord servers pay for a private arena. $9/event.

11 — Virality Hooks

How it spreads

The card share

Card as a styled image. People share their Fatal Flaw on Twitter. The brutally accurate ones go viral on their own.

The roast comment

After voting, leave a one-liner. Best roasts surface publicly. People share screenshots of being roasted.

The challenge link

"@friend I challenged your card. Settle it." Direct battle challenges sent as links. Creates personal stakes.

Group intake

Teachers, team leads, friends use it together. "Do EgoArena with your team" content creates organic B2B discovery.
